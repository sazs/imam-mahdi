Who is Imam Mahdi?

As we all know that Hazrat Imam Mahdi is one of the greatest signs, before the last hour.(Qiayamah) and is one of famous personalities among muslims who are eagerly waiting for his coming.

Many people across the world had claimed to be Mahdi but we eventually come across that they were liars. due to this, the muslims got a mindset that anyone claiming Mahdi would be false only and the true one's coming is very far

But such thinking is not appropiate. If Prophet (SAW) has given the news of Mahdi's (AS) arrival then he will surely come & it is our duty to recognize him and help him as Prophet Muhammad (SAW) said in one sahih hadith: " Helping Mahdi or following his orders is must for every muslim." - (Abu Dawood)

Now the question arises as how to identify the right Mahdi & to realize the time of his coming(appearance)??

The answer to this can only be found from the person who has informed the people about Mahdi's coming i.e Prophet Muhammad (SAW) , the last prophet of this world. So from his words, we can know the details of Mahdi.

We know that the words Prophet Muhammad (SAW) are collected in the form Hadith; so in order to know about hazrat Mahdi (A.S), first we should have good knowledge about hadith it's type, it's origin etc...

Physical Appearance

Hadith-

The Prophet (SAW) said: " The Mahdi will be of my stock, and will have a broad forehead a prominent nose. He will fill the earth will equity and justice as it was filled with oppression and tyranny, and he will rule for seven years. " ( Abu Dawood)

The Prophet (SAW) said, " His(mahdi) nose will be pointed and his face will be bright." (Hakeem)

The above hadiths show that mahdi(as) will have broad forehead, pointed nose and bright face. So the things which are matching are considered and the rest are rejected because to identify someone, only sahih hadith’s narration is the correct basis which are mentioned in the above hadiths.

HIS NAME AND HIS PARENTS

HADITH –

The Prophet (SAW) said: " If only one day of this world remained. Allah would lengthen that day, till He raised up in it a man who belongs to me or to my family whose father's name is the same as my father's, who will fill the earth with equity and justice as it has been filled with oppression and tyranny. " ( Abu Dawood)

The Prophet (SAW) said: " His name will be compatible with mine, and his father’s name with my father’s name... " ( Abu Dawood & Tirmizi)

Inference:

AS WE KNOW THAT, NAME OF A PERSON ISN’T FOUND IN GENERAL IN REGARD TO PROPHECIES. THE THING WE CAN GET IS HAVING ITS MEANING OR ITS QUALITATIVE NAME. HAZUR SAW HAS NOWHERE SAID IN ANY SAHIH HADITH THAT MAHDI’S NAME WILL BE MUHAMMAD BUT HAS SAID THAT HIS NAME WILL BE SAME AS MY NAME. HAZUR SAW’S NAME WAS MUHAMMAD FROM CHILHOOD. BUT ISA (AS) HAS PROPHECISED HIM WITH THE NAME “AHMAD” AS WE SEE IT IN CHAPTER SAF OF QURAN. AHMAD MEANS “THE PRAISED ONE” AND MUHAMMAD ALSO MEANS THE SAME. HENCE THIS PROPHECY BY ISA (AS) IS FULFILLED BY MEANING. THERE ARE ALSO MANY QUALITATIVE NAMES OF HAZUR SAW. SO MAHDI’S NAME COULD BE EITHER MUHAMMAD, AHMAD OR THESE COULD BE INCLUDED IN HIS NAME AS WE SEE THIS IN INDIA THAT PARENTS MOSTLY KEEP MUHAMMAD OR AHMAD AS THEIR SON’S NAME AND TO DIFFERENTIATE ONE FROM THE OTHER THEY ADD A QUALITATIVE NAME TO THIS OR IF THEY ARE NOT AWARE OF THIS FACT, THEN THEY ADD ANY NAME TO DIFFERENTIATE ONE FROM THE OHER. EX- MUHAMMAD SALIM, MUHAMMAD SALMAN, AFROZ AHMAD, ETC.. SO IF MAHDI(AS) IS TO COME FROM INDIA THEN HE COULD ALSO HAVE SUCH NAME. AS FAR AS HIS FATHER’S NAME IS CONCERNED, NOWHERE HAS HAZUR SAW SAID THAT MAHDI’S FATHER’S NAME WILL BE “ABDULLAH” BUT HE SAW SAID THAT MAHDI’S FATHER’S NAME WILL BE SAME AS HIS FATHER’S NAME. ABDULLAHWAS THE REAL FATHER OF HAZUR SAW BUT ON MANY OCCASIONS HAZUR SAW CALLED HAZRAT IBRAHIM AS HIS FATHER AS HAZUR SAW BELONGS TO IBRAHIM (AS) PROGENY. THE VARIOUS HADITHS CALLING IBRAHIM (AS) AS HIS FATHER ARE AS FOLLOWS :- 1. HAZUR SAW SAID,” I AM THE PRODUCT OF MY FATHER IBRAHIM’S (AS) SUPPLICATION,” 2. ON THE OCCASION OF EID-UL-ZOHA, HAZUR SAW SAID,” THIS(SACRIFICE OF EID-UL-ZOHA) IS THE TRADITION OF MY FATHER IBRAHIM(AS).” 3. ON REFERRIN A PARTICULAR SUPPLICATION, HAZUR SAW,” THIS SUPPLICATION WAS DONE BY MY FATHER IBRAHIM(AS)….. ON HIS TWO SONS.” ALSO, WHEN HAZUR SAW WENT ON THE SEVEN HEAVENS ON THE NIGHT OF MERAJ, THEN EVERY PROPHET GREETED HI BY SAYING, “ WELCOME ! RIGHTEOUS PROPHET “ BUT IBRAHIM(AS) SAID, “WELCOME ! RIGHTEOUS PROPHET, RIGHTEOUS SON”. SO FROM THESE POINTS WE COME TO KNOW THAT MAHDI’S FATHER NAME COULD BE ABDULLAH OR IBRAHIM(AS HAZUR SAW CALLED HIM HIS FATHER) OR ANY QUALITATIVE NAME OF IBRAHIM(AS) OR NAMES SIMILAR IN MEANING TO ABDULLAH OR IBRAHIM. IBRAHIM(AS) HAS TWO QUALITATIVE NAME BY WHICH ALLAH HAS CALLED HIM IN QURAN. THEY ARE KHALILULLAH AND HANIF. SO MAHDI’S FATHER’S NAME COULD BE ANY OF THESE TWO NAMES ALSO.
